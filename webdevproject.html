Here are some things that I really enjoy about golf:

  1. It is fun to play.
    • You get to be outside in the sunshine
    • It is competitive
    • You get to challenge yourself mentally and physically
  2. You get to build relationships.
    • You can play with your friends
    • You can play with your family
    • You can even go golfing for work
  3. It is relaxing.
    • Although golf can be challenging, just being outdoors is peaceful
    • A few good golf shots in a row can really boost your self-esteem and make you feel good
